# McCare Global ATS — Trainer Checklist Sheet

## 1) Pre-Session Setup (24–48 Hours Before)
- [ ] Confirm environment URL and login access for trainer account.
- [ ] Prepare at least 2 demo users (Recruiter, Compliance/Admin).
- [ ] Verify sample records exist:
  - [ ] 3+ leads in different pipeline stages
  - [ ] 2+ candidates
  - [ ] 3+ documents with mixed status
  - [ ] 1 facility + 1 job order
  - [ ] 1 assignment + 1 timesheet (draft/submitted)
- [ ] Confirm browser compatibility and stable internet at training location.
- [ ] Share meeting invite with agenda and expected duration.

## 3) Trainer Run-of-Show (45–60 Minutes)

### A. Orientation (5 min)
- [ ] Show sidebar modules in order.
- [ ] Explain team ownership by module.

### B. Lead Workflow (10–12 min)
- [ ] Leads → Add Lead
- [ ] Move lead stage
- [ ] Convert lead to candidate

### C. Candidate + Compliance (10–12 min)
- [ ] Candidates → open profile/tabs
- [ ] Compliance → Verify one document
- [ ] Explain expiring/expired risk handling

### D. Jobs + Assignments (10–12 min)
- [ ] Clients & Jobs → Create Job Order
- [ ] Assignments → Create Assignment

### E. Timesheets (8–10 min)
- [ ] Timesheets → create or open draft
- [ ] Submit then approve workflow

### F. Wrap-Up (3–5 min)
- [ ] Recap end-to-end flow
- [ ] Confirm next-step owners and timeline

## 4) Click-by-Click Demo Prompts (Trainer Cue Cards)

### Lead Intake to Conversion
1. Click **Leads**.
2. Click **Add Lead**.
3. Complete required fields and save.
4. Open lead menu (**...**) and move stage.
5. Open lead menu (**...**) and click **Convert to Candidate**.

### Compliance Verification
1. Click **Compliance**.
2. Locate a **Pending** document.
3. Click **Verify**.
4. Call out status change and impact.

### Job to Assignment
1. Click **Clients & Jobs**.
2. Open **Job Orders** tab.
3. Click **Create Job Order** and save.
4. Click **Assignments**.
5. Click **Create Assignment** and save.

### Timesheet Approval
1. Click **Timesheets**.
2. Open a draft/submitted timesheet.
3. Click **Submit** (if draft).
4. Click **Approve** (if submitted).

## 5) Common Questions & Suggested Answers
- **Q: Who owns each module?**
  - A: Recruiters (Leads/Candidates), Compliance (Documents), Operations (Jobs/Assignments), Scheduler/Finance (Timesheets), Admin (settings).
- **Q: How do we reduce compliance risk?**
  - A: Monitor status transitions and act on Expiring Soon/Expired before placements start.
- **Q: What should leadership watch weekly?**
  - A: conversion velocity, credential risk counts, fill rate, timesheet approval speed.
