# McCare Global ATS — Executive Onboarding Deck (10 Minutes)

## Slide 1 — Welcome & Objective (0:00–0:45)
**Title:** From Lead Intake to Billing in One ATS Workflow

**Speaker notes:**
- Welcome and thank stakeholders for joining.
- Set outcome: clear understanding of business flow, leadership visibility, and next rollout steps.
- Position this as an operational platform, not just a recruiting database.

---

## Slide 2 — Business Lifecycle Overview (0:45–2:00)
**Title:** The 5-Phase Recruitment Cycle

**Slide bullets:**
- Lead Capture
- Candidate Onboarding
- Job Matching
- Assignment Placement
- Timesheet & Billing

**Speaker notes:**
- Emphasize that this mirrors real staffing operations end-to-end.
- Mention reduced handoff friction and better accountability between teams.

---

## Slide 3 — Role-Based Navigation (2:00–3:00)
**Title:** Modules by Team Function

**Slide bullets:**
- Recruiters: Leads, Candidates
- Compliance: Compliance, Documents
- Operations: Clients & Jobs, Assignments
- Scheduling/Finance: Timesheets, Reports
- Admin controls: Lead Capture settings, Notification settings

**Speaker notes:**
- Explain that each module supports a distinct ownership area.
- Note that admin-only areas centralize governance and system controls.

---

## Slide 4 — Lead Pipeline Visibility (3:00–4:15)
**Title:** Structured Lead Progression

**Slide bullets:**
- Standardized stages from New Lead to Hired/Rejected
- Better forecasting of recruiting throughput
- Consistent stage movement and auditability

**Speaker notes:**
- Explain why standard stages improve team consistency.
- Tie pipeline discipline to conversion performance.

---

## Slide 5 — Candidate Readiness & Risk (4:15–5:15)
**Title:** Onboarding + Compliance in One Flow

**Slide bullets:**
- Candidate profile completeness supports matching quality
- Credential statuses: Pending, Verified, Expiring Soon, Expired
- Early warning reduces placement risk

**Speaker notes:**
- Frame compliance as a risk-control layer, not just a checklist.

---

## Slide 6 — Demand to Placement (5:15–6:30)
**Title:** Jobs, Facilities, and Assignments

**Slide bullets:**
- Create and track job orders
- Match and place candidates with contract/rate details
- Monitor assignment status and readiness

**Speaker notes:**
- Describe this as connecting client demand directly to fill activity.

---

## Slide 7 — Timesheet to Billing Control (6:30–7:30)
**Title:** Operational Close Loop

**Slide bullets:**
- Status progression: Draft → Submitted → Approved → Processed
- Clear approval chain for scheduling/finance
- Billing-ready operational data

**Speaker notes:**
- Stress compliance, timeliness, and financial control.

---

## Slide 8 — Executive KPI Lens (7:30–9:00)
**Title:** What Leadership Should Track Weekly

**Slide bullets:**
- Lead conversion velocity
- Candidate readiness ratio
- Expiring credentials risk count
- Assignment fill rate
- Timesheet approval turnaround

**Speaker notes:**
- Explain that these KPIs tie directly to revenue reliability and service quality.

---

## Slide 9 — Adoption Plan (9:00–10:00)
**Title:** 30-Day Rollout Recommendation

**Slide bullets:**
- Week 1: Role onboarding and baseline workflows
- Week 2: Data quality and compliance discipline
- Week 3: Throughput tuning and KPI cadence
- Week 4: Executive review and SOP lock-in

**Speaker notes:**
- Close with clear accountability and training continuation plan.
