## Common Workflows

### Add New Lead
`Leads` → `Add Lead` → Fill form → `Add Lead`

### Convert Lead to Candidate
`Leads` → Find lead → `...` menu → `Convert to Candidate`

### Verify Document
`Compliance` → Find document → `Verify` button

### Create Job Order
`Clients & Jobs` → `Job Orders` tab → `Create Job Order` → Fill form → `Create`

### Create Assignment
`Assignments` → `Create Assignment` → Select candidate, facility, dates → `Create`

### Submit Timesheet
`Timesheets` → Find draft timesheet → `Submit` button

### Approve Timesheet
`Timesheets` → Find submitted timesheet → `Approve` button

## Billing Formula

```
Regular Pay = Regular Hours × Pay Rate
OT Pay = OT Hours × Pay Rate × 1.5
Total Pay = Regular Pay + OT Pay

Regular Bill = Regular Hours × Bill Rate
OT Bill = OT Hours × Bill Rate × 1.5
Total Billable = Regular Bill + OT Bill
```
